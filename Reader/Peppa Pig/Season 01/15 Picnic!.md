# Picnic!

 66. Oh.

 67. You are very lucky ducks.

 68. Say ''thank you'' to Daddy Pig.

 69. You're most welcome.

 70. It's time to go home.

 71. Say goodbye to the ducks.

 72. Bye bye, ducks.

 73. See you next time!
