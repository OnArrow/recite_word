# Lesson 55

1. 如果在姓氏后面加`s`，前面加定冠词`the`，用来指一家人。特别是丈夫和妻子

   ```
   the Sawyers
   索耶一家

   the Smiths
   史密斯一家

   the Georges
   乔治一家
   ```

2. `live`

   ```
   live in + 国家/城市
   live at + 门牌号

   I live in Nanning

   I live at 99
   ```

3. 在中午可以说 `at noon`，也可以说 `at midday`

   ```
   I usually drink milk at noon

   The sun always shines at midday

   He sometimes sleeps in his office at midday
   ```
